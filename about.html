About Analyst44

Analyst44 is a professional-grade market intelligence platform designed to transform earnings volatility into structured, actionable trading insight through real-time scoring and anomaly detection.

Structured Financial Insight

Analyst44 converts fragmented earnings releases into clean, normalized financial signals, allowing consistent comparison across companies and quarters.

Real-Time Earnings Scoring

Automated pipelines generate 0–100 earnings scores within seconds of release, reflecting surprise, quality, and forward implications.

Designed for Speed

Built for traders and analysts who require low-latency insight, minimal noise, and decision-ready data without manual processing.

Why Professionals Choose Analyst44

Analyst44 is used by active traders, research desks, and quantitative teams who need earnings data that is faster, cleaner, and more interpretable than headlines or raw filings.

  • ✔ Consistent, comparable financial fields
  • ✔ Instant earnings impact scoring
  • ✔ Automated anomaly and deviation detection
  • ✔ No manual data normalization
  • ✔ Built for institutional-grade workflows
